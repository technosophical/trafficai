Why Acoustic Monitoring?

Traditional traffic monitoring methods like video cameras are expensive, complex, and raise privacy concerns. Ecoustics uses sound to deliver fast, cost-effective, and reliable insights for rural and urban environments alike.

Advantages Over Traditional Methods:

  • Lower Cost: Simple hardware and installation.
  • Privacy Respecting: No visual data or identifying information.
  • Resilient: Works in low-light, fog, or remote conditions.
  • Lightweight: Ideal for rural traffic monitoring where cameras are impractical.
